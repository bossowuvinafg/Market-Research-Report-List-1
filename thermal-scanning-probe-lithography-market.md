<p><p>Thermal Scanning Probe Lithography (TSPL) is an advanced nanofabrication technique that utilizes a heated atomic force microscope probe to locally modify materials at the nanoscale. By applying heat through the probe, TSPL can induce chemical changes in resist materials, allowing for the precise creation of nanostructures with high resolution and minimal collateral damage. This method is particularly advantageous for applications in semiconductor manufacturing, data storage, and nanoelectronics.</p><p>The Thermal Scanning Probe Lithography Market is witnessing significant growth, driven by increasing demand for miniaturization in electronics and advancements in nanotechnology.
